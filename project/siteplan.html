Purpose for QualityOfJavaMC

The purpose of QualityOfJavaMC is to provide a platform for discovering and commissioning safe high-quality Java Edition Minecraft mods.

Scenarios

  • What are some good Minecraft Java mods?
  • How can I have my own mod commissioned?
  • What kind of mods can I discover on QualityOFJavaMC?
  • How do I know if a mod is high-quality and safe to use?
